Cookies Notice — Veridian Urban Systems

Veridian Urban Systems (“VUS,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies to maintain platform security, improve performance, analyze usage patterns, and deliver a reliable user experience. This notice explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and the choices available to you.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website or access our platform. They help us recognize your browser, remember your preferences, and keep the platform functioning smoothly.
We also use technologies that operate like cookies—such as local storage, session storage, and tracking pixels. These serve similar functions and are included in this notice.

2. Why We Use Cookies

We rely on cookies for several core purposes:

Essential Operations

These cookies allow the platform to work. They support login functions, maintain session integrity, enable security features, and keep the system stable.

Performance and Analytics

We use analytics tools to understand how the platform is accessed and used. This helps us improve system performance, troubleshoot issues, and refine the quality of insights produced by VUS.

Preferences

Some cookies store basic user settings—such as language or display preferences—to create a consistent experience each time you log in.

Security and Fraud Prevention

Cookies assist in detecting abnormal activity, preventing unauthorized access, and protecting the integrity of our systems. We do not use cookies for targeted advertising or commercial tracking.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Certain trusted service providers may place cookies on our platform to support analytics, security, and performance monitoring. These providers are bound by strict confidentiality and data-protection obligations.

4. Your Choices

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block or restrict cookies
  • Delete cookies already stored
  • Configure alerts before cookies are placed

If you disable essential cookies, parts of the platform may not function properly.
